Visions To Reality

Vision 2 Reality (V2R) empowers youth and small businesses through hands-on marketing, sales, and business training.

  • Students—especially underserved and at-risk youth—learn real-world skills by working directly with small businesses.
  • Businesses get affordable campaigns, market research, social media support, and sales strategies that help them grow.

Together, we're building stronger youth, stronger businesses, and stronger communities.

Our Mission

At V2R and ABM, LLC, we are driven by a shared passion for empowering individuals and communities through the transformative power of mindset and skills. Our organization was founded on the belief that creativity, resilience, and opportunity are the cornerstones of success. Drawing from our collective experiences overcoming challenges, we have built a platform that equips people with the tools to thrive.

V2R goes beyond teaching marketing skills & expertise; we cultivate confidence, foster a growth-oriented mindset, and inspire a vision for lifelong achievement. We believe that a strong mindset is the foundation for building vibrant, thriving communities. By empowering youth—particularly those from underserved backgrounds—and supporting small businesses, we create meaningful opportunities that benefit everyone involved.

Together, we are shaping a brighter future, one empowered individual and community at a time. It's a win-win that strengthens everyone involved.

How V2R Works

Phase 1 – Launching Now (2025)

Online training courses in marketing, sales, and social media.

Business case studies where students and instructors help local businesses.

Affordable campaigns for underserved businesses (ads, content, sales strategies).

Mentorship and professional references for students.

Phase 2 – Coming in 2026

Experiential event training & apprenticeship programs: students work on event creation in various roles and as brand ambassadors at fairs, expos, conferences, and other community events, giving businesses visibility and youth hands-on confidence.
